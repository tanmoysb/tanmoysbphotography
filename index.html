About Me

Hi, My name is Tanmoy sb. I am a photographer. I am a professional photographer. I love taking pictures. In the beginning, photography was a hobby, but later I gradually made it my profession. I love to get things done in a very perfect way. So far I have completed over 20 events that were wedding events. I also do fashion photoshoots. There is also an indoor photoshoot.
I want to capture the beautiful moments of people through this profession in a frame so that they can remember these memories very clearly by looking at these pictures after marriage. My only ambition is that I want to give people beautiful pictures through this photography skill.
